1. Introduction

Welcome to Nose Roast ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Nose Roast collects, uses, shares, and protects your information.

📷

2. Camera & Facial Data

Nose Roast uses your device's camera strictly for gameplay mechanics — controlling the in-game character using your nose via Google MediaPipe face tracking.

✅  All facial detection processing occurs locally on your device in real-time and is immediately discarded. No biometric data leaves your device.

  • We do not record, store, or transmit any images or video
  • We do not perform facial recognition or store face prints
  • We do not share any camera data with third parties
💾

3. Local Device Storage

We use your device's local storage solely to save game progress between sessions.

🔒  This data remains on your device permanently and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

The only data stored locally is:

  • Your in-game high score
  • Your selected username (for generating shareable scores locally)
📢

4. Advertising Data

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain information to provide personalised ads or for analytics.

⚙️  You may opt out of personalised advertising via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and usage data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

🚸

5. Children's Privacy

⚠️  Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.
